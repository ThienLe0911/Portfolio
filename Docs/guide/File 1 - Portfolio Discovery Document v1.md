# Portfolio Discovery Document

## Purpose

This portfolio is not intended to be a traditional online resume.

Its primary goal is to communicate the engineering journey, product impact, and growth mindset of Le Hoang Thien to engineering managers, recruiters, and product companies.

The portfolio should answer three questions:

1. What kinds of products has he built?
2. What engineering challenges has he solved?
3. What direction is he growing toward?

The portfolio should not feel like a list of technologies.

Instead, it should feel like the story of an engineer evolving from frontend development into software engineering through real-world product experience.
